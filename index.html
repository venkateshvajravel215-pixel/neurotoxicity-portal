What is Neurotoxicity?

Neurotoxicity occurs when exposure to natural or manmade toxic substances (neurotoxicants) alters the normal activity of the nervous system.

Key Biological Markers

  • Neuronal cell death (Apoptosis)
  • Synaptic dysfunction
  • Oxidative stress in glial cells
